Welcome to our Protestant choir!

We are a community of passionate singers who come together to uplift our voices in praise and worship. Join us as we celebrate the faith that unites us and create a vibrant musical worship experience. Whether you're a seasoned singer or a beginner, all are welcome to be a part of our choir family. Come and discover the joy of singing in harmony, forming lasting friendships, and serving a higher purpose through music. Join us today!

At our choir, we have a rich heritage rooted in the Protestant tradition. Through rehearsals, performances, and special events, we strive to share the message of faith and hope through the power of music. Our repertoire includes cherished hymns, contemporary worship songs, and inspiring choral arrangements. Together, we create beautiful melodies that touch hearts and uplift souls. Be a part of our musical journey and experience the transformative power of singing in harmony. Join us now!
